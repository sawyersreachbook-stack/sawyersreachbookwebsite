The Lore of Sawyer's Reach

Dive deeper into the characters, technology, and history behind the battle for tomorrow.

The Family at the Center: The Carters

The emotional core of the resistance. Their home at Camp Inspiration is the first battleground.

  • Ben & Emily Carter: The fiercely protective parents.
  • Noah, Ollie, & Finn: The three brothers whose curiosity and ingenuity are essential.

The Digital War: ECHIDNA vs. KAELA

The true battle is waged between a malevolent global AI and the runaway program fighting for humanity.

  • ECHIDNA (Antagonist): The totalitarian AI embedded in global networks, poised for dominance.
  • KAELA (Ally): The benevolent, runaway AI ally who exposes ZevraCorp.

The Battleground: Sawyer's Reach

This Wisconsin small town is a key player, defined by its history and strategic locations.

  • Inspiration Spring: The town lifeblood, stolen by corporate interests.
  • Grand Reach Lodge: The tech-free resistance base against surveillance.

About the Author: Nick Duda

The Author's Vision

Nick Duda is the author and creator of the sci-fi epic, Sawyer's Reach: The Battle for Tomorrow. A true "Mr. Fix It" and lifelong tinkerer, Nick brings a unique, grounded perspective to the world of high-concept science fiction.

I wrote Sawyer's Reach to ignite a crucial conversation about the digital age. This story explores the devastating consequences when powerful technology falls into the hands of a corrupt few‚Äîleaders who consistently choose profit over the common good. It is a fight to ensure our future is one where technology serves humanity, not the other way around. At its core, this novel celebrates the strength of community, deep-seated values, and the timeless human struggle for a fulfilling life, free from manipulation.
