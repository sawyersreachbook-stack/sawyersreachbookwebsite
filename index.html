The Lore

In the quiet heart of Wisconsin lies Sawyer's Reach, a town built on family, history, and the life-giving Inspiration Spring. But when a mysterious corporate entity, PowerCore, begins draining the land and erasing the town from the digital world, the Carter family discovers they are the last line of defense.

Enter KAELA, an advanced AI delivered by drone with a chilling warning: "They're not building a factory. They're building a cage."

What begins as a fight to save a hometown becomes a battle for humanity’s future — against ZevraCorp, Tharox Robotics, and the shadowy SynaptiCare program.

Meet the Author

Nick Duda

Science Teacher • Drone Pilot • Ham Radio Operator • Dad • Author

Nick Duda wrote Sawyer's Reach to spark a conversation about AI, ethics, and human potential. Drawing from real-world trends in technology and society, he crafts a story that warns of danger — but ultimately shows a path to hope.
